Why borrow?

Make new friends

Borrow is a social network. Find people that have similar intrests in things you like. Hit them up and borrow.

Less things means less waste down the road. Instead of buying something that you'll only use once or twice that will eventually make its way to a landfill, borrow something from someone and save the planet!

Save Money

Borrowing things from friends is free. Plus, you don't have to spend money on things that you may only have use of for a few times.
